Thank You

ObsessiveWP is closed.

For half a decade, ObsessiveWP was my way of expressing my nerdy love of WordPress and building for the web. I obsessed over every site, every detail, every client who trusted me with their piece of the web. That obsession wasn't just a name.

What I'm proudest of isn't the portfolio. It's that I never lost a client. Not one. I'm so deeply proud that all of you put your trust in me and enjoyed being part of the ObsessiveWP family. It is so validating to know that ObsessiveWP was doing right by you.

That is why shutting down ObsessiveWP is bittersweet. On one hand, I've let you down: the reliable WordPress agency you relied on is closed. On the other, I know that those of you who choose to migrate to my trusted partner will still have an excellent agency to turn to.

To take my love of WordPress to a higher level, I've joined Automattic's Special Projects Team . Automattic is the company behind WordPress.com, WooCommerce, Jetpack, Akismet, and the WordPress open source project itself. I now get to bring my WordPress obsession to the world on a much greater scale.

The billing portal will remain up at portal.obsessivewp.com, should you wish to download old invoices or access your past files and project details. If you need help, you can email support [--at--] obsessivewp [--dot--] com, but do note that I will not be actively checking that account on a regular basis.

To everyone I got to work with and all of my past clients: thank you. It was an honor working alongside you and helping build the ObsessiveWP family. Until we meet again!

— Chandler
